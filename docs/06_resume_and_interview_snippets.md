## Technical Interview Answer

The architecture separates workflow coordination from business-agent logic. The workflow runtime owns step order and state. Agents own business tasks. Shared context carries workflow state between steps, while outputs and traces make the process reviewable. This avoids the common problem of disconnected AI scripts that are difficult to debug, explain, or extend.

## Product-Focused Interview Answer
